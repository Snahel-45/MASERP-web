Services Offered by MASERP Technical Services LLP

At MASERP Technical Services LLP, we provide end-to-end steel detailing solutions using industry-leading software like Tekla Structures 2021, SDS-2, and AutoCAD. Our expertise ensures accurate, fabrication-ready deliverables that meet global quality standards and project timelines.

Steel Detailing Services

We deliver high-precision steel detailing services for structural and miscellaneous components including beams, columns, trusses, stairs, handrails, platforms, and more. Our detailed models and drawings support smooth fabrication and erection.

Shop Drawing Services

Our shop drawings include detailed views, sections, and connection details required for fabrication and field erection. These drawings are generated directly from accurate 3D models to avoid clashes and minimize site issues.

Steel Detail Work

From structural analysis to component-level details, our team ensures every steel member is correctly modeled and represented in drawings. This guarantees efficient production, accurate fitting, and structural safety.

Steel Design Detailing

We collaborate with engineers and architects to translate design concepts into constructible models and drawings. Our design detailing ensures each steel element aligns with structural integrity and design intent.
